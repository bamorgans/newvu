# NewVu Product Vision

NewVu is a modern AI-assisted interview platform designed as a creative studio rather than a traditional recruiting system.

## Experience Goals

- Video-first interview workspace
- Glass interface with floating controls
- AI interview assistant
- Real-time quote extraction
- Film editor inspired review workflow
- Responsive mobile-first experience

## Core Workflows

1. Interview pipeline
2. Live interview
3. AI assisted evaluation
4. Video review studio
